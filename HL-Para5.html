The Moneylender forgiving unequal debts

Luke 7:41-43

41"Two people owed money to a certain moneylender. One owed him five hundred denarii, and the other fifty. 42Neither of them had the money to pay him back, so he forgave the debts of both. Now which of them will love him more?" 43Simon replied, "I suppose the one who had the bigger debt forgiven."
